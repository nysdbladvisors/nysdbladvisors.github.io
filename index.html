Did you know you can use your work-related benefits for illnesses or accidents not caused at work? e.g., a fall at home or somewhere other than work. If you have a work-related injury, you must apply for benefits with the Workers' Compensation Board.

https://www.wcb.ny.gov/content/main/Workers/Workers.jsp

If you have a medical condition that prevents you from working, this benefit provides you with salary coverage while you recover and return to work.

Did you know you can use your paid family leave benefits to care for a family member and receive salary coverage while you are away from work caring for your family member?
